Hello, I am Fathi Salem.
I am a faculty in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research areas of interest have evolved over the years in the general areas of Dynamic and Adaptive Systems and Circuits. These areas include the domain of Deep Neural Networks or Deep Learning.

My current focus is on (gated and ungated) recurrent neural networks with efficient architectural implmentations. In addition to tangible computational savings, these architectures are more suitable for embedded platforms and mobile devices-- with comparatively limited resources.
